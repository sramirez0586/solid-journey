Real Policy Solutions

Protecting abortion rights requires **bold action**, not legislative half-measures. As Noya Rimalt argues, abortion laws based on compromise often fail to secure reproductive freedom.

  • Enact Federal Legislation: Codify abortion protections into law.
  • Strengthen State Laws: Ensure protections in states where abortion is still legal.
  • Repeal Harmful Restrictions: Remove bans and waiting period laws.
  • Invest in Healthcare Access: Fund clinics and telemedicine for abortion care.
